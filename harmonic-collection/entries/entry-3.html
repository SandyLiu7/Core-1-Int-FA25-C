Cantonese Herbal Sweet Soup

Cantonese Herbal Sweet Soup is one of the traditional sweet soups in Guangdong, originated from the Lingnan region, and is especially popular in summer. The words "qing", "bu" and "liang" in its name represent three major characteristics: clearing heat and relieving summer heat, nourishing the body and having a refreshing taste when entering the mouth. Common ingredients include lotus seeds, lily bulbs, red dates, coix seed, silver ear fungus, longan, polygonatum, etc. They are not only food ingredients but also commonly used medicinal and edible products in traditional Chinese medicine. Cantonese Herbal Sweet Soup emphasizes a balanced combination. It is neither too cold to hurt the stomach nor causes internal heat like traditional tonics. It can not only serve as a dessert to cool off in summer but also as a daily food therapy for nourishment and health preservation. Therefore, it has become a common classic in Cantonese sweet soup shops and households.

Ingredients

Milk + coconut water (2:1 ratio)

Honey (or substitute with coconut milk for a stronger coconut flavor)

1 slice watermelon

1 mango

A handful of mung beans

A small handful of raisins

Taro balls

Rock sugar

3â€“4 Chinese red dates (or crispy jujube slices)

Instructions

1. Cook the mung beans: Soak the mung beans overnight. For a creamier texture, freeze them for a few hours before cooking. Boil for about 20 minutes, adding a few pieces of rock sugar halfway through.

2. Cook the taro balls: Drop the taro balls into boiling water. Once they float, cook for another 5 minutes. Turn off the heat, cover, and let sit for 5 minutes. Drain and cool them in cold water.

3. Prepare the base: Mix milk and coconut water in a 2:1 ratio, then sweeten with honey. Alternatively, use coconut milk directly for a richer taste.

4. Prepare the fruits: Cut watermelon, mango, and red dates into small cubes.

5. Assemble: In a bowl, add the cooked mung beans, taro balls, watermelon, mango, and raisins.

6. Finish: Pour in the coconut milk mixture and enjoy this refreshing, nourishing dessert.
