Lotus Seed, Lily Bulb & White Fungus Sweet Soup

Instructions

1.Soak the dried white fungus (Tremella) in cold water for 1â€“2 hours until fully expanded. Tear it into small pieces.

2.Soak lotus seeds and lily bulbs separately for about 30 minutes.

3.In a large pot, add all ingredients except goji berries. Pour in enough water to fully submerge them.

4.Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer slowly for at least 1 hour.

5.Add the goji berries in the last 10 minutes of cooking.

6.Once the soup becomes thick and gelatinous, remove from heat and serve warm or chilled.

Lotus Seed, Lily Bulb & White Fungus sweet soup is a classic Chinese health-preserving sweet soup, renowned for its warmth and nourishment. White Fungus is rich in plant collagen and has the effects of nourishing Yin, moistening the lungs, beautifying the complexion and moisturizing the skin. Lotus seeds can nourish the spleen and heart, calm the mind and help with sleep. Lily Bulb can clear the mind, moisten the lungs and relieve anxiety. Red Dates and longans benefit qi and nourish blood. Goji berries brighten the complexion and enhance physical strength. The entire bowl of sugar is warm and smooth, with a natural sweet aroma and gelatinous texture. It is not only a dessert but also a traditional form of food therapy - regulating blood and qi, nourishing the body and mind, and balancing the constitution.

Ingredients

35 g dried white fungus (Tremella)

30 g dried lotus seeds

20 g dried lily bulbs

8 dried red dates (pitted)

8 dried longans

A small handful of goji berries

Water (enough to cover all ingredients generously)
