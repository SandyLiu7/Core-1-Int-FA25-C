Papaya Stewed with Peach Gum

Instructions

1.Soak the peach gum in water for at least 12 hours until no hard core remains.

2.Place the soaked peach gum in a pot with cold water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 30 minutes.

3.Add diced papaya and rock sugar. Continue simmering for another 10 minutes.

4.Before serving, pour in milk and stir well. Serve warm or chilled.

Papaya Stewed with Peach Gum is a traditional Chinese dessert with a refreshing taste and rich gum. The main ingredient, Peach Gum, has the effects of promoting blood circulation, removing blood stasis, nourishing Yin and beautifying the complexion, but it is not suitable for pregnant women to consume. Papaya is sweet and delicious, which is beneficial for digestion and beauty care. The natural sweetness of old Rock Sugar is mild. The taste becomes smoother and richer after adding Milk. This dessert is not only sweet and delicious in taste, but also has the effect of food therapy - moistening dryness and nourishing the body. It is especially suitable for people who are dry or need to replenish water and maintain beauty.

Ingredients

20 g peach gum

20 g rock sugar

150 g papayar

150 ml milk

Water (enough to cover the peach gum)
