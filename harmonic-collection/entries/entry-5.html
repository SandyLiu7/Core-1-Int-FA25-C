Sugarcane and Imperatae Root Drink

Instructions

1.Wash all ingredients thoroughly.

2.Add all ingredients to a pot with enough water to fully submerge them.

3.Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for about 20 minutes.

4.Serve warm or chilled as desired.

Tip: For convenience, busy parents can use a health pot / electric steamer instead of stovetop simmeringâ€”quick and easy!

Sugarcane and Imperatae Root Drink is a traditional Chinese health drink, which has the effects of clearing heat, moistening the lungs, generating body fluids and relieving summer heat. The main ingredient, Sugarcane, is sweet and refreshing, which can relieve thirst and summer heat. Imperatae Root has the functions of clearing heat, promoting diuresis, reducing internal heat and relieving summer heat. Sea Coconut moistens the lungs and clears heat. Figs and Dried pears complement the natural sweetness while alleviating the heat and dryness. The entire drink has a refreshing and slightly sweet taste, making it suitable for consumption in summer. It not only quenches thirst but also helps clear the internal heat. If you want to save time, this drink can also be quickly made in a health pot/electric steamer, which is perfect for busy daily life.

Ingredients

3 small segments of sugarcane

1 small section of imperatae root

10 g dried sea coconut

2 dried figs

2 slices of dried pear

Water (enough to cover ingredients)
